## 1ページ目プロンプト

```text
Create a SINGLE-PAGE vertical Japanese manga illustration. CRITICAL: Output must be PORTRAIT orientation (aspect ratio 2:3). LAYOUT: FULL BLEED (Extend art to all edges). DO NOT leave white margins, especially on the right side. Fill the entire canvas width.

### Technical Setup:
- Aspect Ratio: 1200:1697 (Portrait)
- Resolution: High quality manga illustration

### Character Anatomy:
- HANDS/FINGERS: MUST be drawing with 5 fingers. Knuckles and joints must be anatomically correct. NO deformities.
- EYES: Symmetrical, properly aligned. Clear pupils.
- FACE: Proportionate features, clean jawline. NO distorted faces.
- BODY: Natural pose and anatomy. Arms and legs must have correct length and joints.

### Character Settings:
- Remi: (SIGNATURE OUTFIT: Always wearing a Tailored RED blazer over a Black lace top, no variations allowed). (Silky SILVER hair:1.5), (Vibrant RED eyes:1.4). NO GLOVES.
- Yuto: Short Black hair, (Traditional Black GAKURAN school uniform:1.4), Gold buttons. Energetic learner. BARE HANDS (no gloves).

### PAGE 2 VERTICAL LAYOUT (1200w x 1697h)
Top 40%: Close-up of Remi holding glowing golden sphere (symbolizing '毎月配当'), face illuminated, wise expression. Speech bubble (Remi): "毎月お小遣いが入る喜び。。 これが投資の本質よ。しっかり頭に叩き込みなさい。"
Middle 30%: Remi in split-world. LEFT: dark storm/chaos (purple/red). RIGHT: golden garden/peace (representing '毎月配当'). She points to golden side decisively.
Bottom 30%: Panel 3 right - Yuto with golden bubbles showing happy future. Panel 4 left - Yuto enlightened. Speech bubble: "そうか…毎月配当の本質はここにあったんですね。" Remi watching warmly (no speech).

### Art Style:
Cinematic lighting, Gold/Purple theme. NO GLOVES.
```
