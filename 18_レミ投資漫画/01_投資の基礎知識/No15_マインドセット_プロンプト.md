【IMAGE_GENERATION_TASK】Generate a high-quality manga illustration BASE ON THE FOLLOWING VISUAL DESCRIPTION. DO NOT OUTPUT ANY TEXT OR CODE. ONLY OUTPUT THE IMAGE.

Panel 1 (Top 40%): VISUAL MANIFESTATION. Close up on Remi holding a small, glowing sphere of light or symbol that represents 'マインドセット'. It shines brighter than the Background elements. She looks gentle and wise. "心が一番大事。テクニックよりメンタル。 これが投資の本質よ。しっかり頭に叩き込みなさい。" (In a speech bubble).
